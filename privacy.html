Privacy Policy

Daily Support Guide is a static education site. During Phase 1A, the site does not use tracking analytics, advertising pixels, affiliate tracking, contact forms, accounts, or newsletter signups.

Quiz answers

The Lifestyle Explorer uses answers only in the visitor's browser to show the next prompt and result. The answers are not stored by the site and are not sent anywhere.

Server logs

The site is hosted through GitHub Pages. Standard hosting logs may be processed by the hosting provider for security, delivery, and abuse prevention.

External links

Phase 1A content avoids retailer links, affiliate links, and monetized outbound links. If this changes in a future phase, this policy and the site disclosures must be updated before launch.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact information listed on the Contact page.
