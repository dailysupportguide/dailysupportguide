About

Daily Support Guide

Daily Support Guide publishes plain, brand-neutral education for everyday routines, label reading, and practical comparison habits.

The site is written for readers in the United States who want calmer ways to organize information before making ordinary daily decisions.

What we do

We explain how to read labels, compare neutral details, and keep notes without turning every decision into a product hunt.

What we do not do

We do not provide medical advice, diagnose conditions, recommend products, rank brands, publish affiliate links, or tell readers what to buy.

Phase 1A boundary

During Phase 1A, the site has no affiliate links, no tracking analytics, no brand recommendations, and no retailer links.
