# Editorial Policy

Daily Support Guide publishes general educational articles for everyday organization, label reading, and comparison habits.

## Allowed

- Explaining how to read a label.
- Comparing formats, serving counts, package sizes, and neutral attributes.
- Building checklists and routines.
- Encouraging readers to verify unclear information.
- Encouraging readers to ask qualified professionals for personal medical questions.

## Not Allowed

- Product recommendations.
- Brand names.
- Retailer names.
- Affiliate links.
- Coupon language.
- Claims that a product treats, cures, prevents, or diagnoses a condition.
- Personalized medical advice.
- Dosage advice.
- Fear-based health claims.

## Required Disclaimer

Each article must include a clear sentence that the article is general education only and is not medical advice.
