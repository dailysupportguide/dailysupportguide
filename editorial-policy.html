Editorial Policy

Useful information, clear limits.

Daily Support Guide publishes general educational content for everyday organization, label reading, and neutral comparison habits.

Allowed topics

Articles may explain label terms, serving sizes, package counts, directions, comparison notes, routines, and practical checklists.

Restricted topics

Articles must not recommend products, name brands, list retailers, publish coupons, provide dosage advice, diagnose conditions, or claim that a product treats, cures, or prevents a disease.

Review process

Before publication, articles must pass internal lint checks and editorial review. Important site tools and approved articles are also reviewed through the four-AI review loop when available.

Corrections

Corrections should preserve the site's neutral scope. If a correction involves health, legal, privacy, or monetization risk, it must be reviewed before publication.
